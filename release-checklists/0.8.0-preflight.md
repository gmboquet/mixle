## Deferred to hosted or external gates

- Strict Sphinx reached 43% before the mandatory 30-second local cutoff. The sandbox also could not resolve
  Python, NumPy, or SciPy intersphinx inventories. Hosted docs CI must finish with warnings treated as errors.
- Twine is not installed in the local environment. The publish workflow installs Twine and must run
  `twine check` against both exact-candidate artifacts before any publication step.
- Mypy is not installed locally. The blocking typed-core hosted job remains required.
- Full, minimum-version, macOS/Linux, extras, optional, real-data, randomized-order, and clean-resolver jobs
  remain hosted evidence; they were deliberately not approximated with a long local suite.
- Independent statistical/systems review, two release testers, external-use-case reproduction, TestPyPI
  configuration, and release-owner sign-off remain external gates.
- CUDA, multi-GPU, multi-node, and numerical performance claims are excluded from 0.8.0 unless designated
  hardware receipts are attached. Historical pre-0.8 measurements are archived and are not release evidence.

## Publication guard

The final preparation PR targets `release/0.8.0`. Do not merge it, create `v0.8.0`, publish to PyPI, or publish
versioned documentation until the remaining hosted and external gates are resolved and the release owner signs
the exact candidate.
